- Share units, par value (if any), controlled appreciation rules

- Annual meeting, special meetings, notice rules

- Budget approval process

## Article VII – Default, Termination, Eviction

- Member default procedures
- Due process and Right-to-Counsel integration

## Article VIII – Special Assessments and Capital Projects

- Approval thresholds
- Rehabilitation governance and tenant protections

## Article IX – Reporting and Transparency

- Quarterly financial statements to members
- Annual independent audit interval

## Article X – Amendments and Dissolution

- Amendment procedure (supermajority)
- Dissolution waterfall consistent with low-income covenant
