# PET_Equity_Share_Agreement_Template

**Title:** PET Equity-Share Agreement

## 1. Parties and Building Description

- Parties: \[City / HPD\] and \[Cooperative/CLT/Building Entity\] and \[Household Member(s)\]
- Building: \[Street address, borough, tax lot, HPD ID\]
- Effective date: \[DATE\]

## 2. Definitions

- "Equity Share"
- "Accrual Rate"
- "Vesting"
- "Default"
- "Exit"
- "Successor"

## 3. Household Obligations

- Monthly payment obligations (rent split / equity contribution)
- Participation requirements (meetings, financial reporting, trainings)
- Maintenance and occupancy rules

## 4. City Obligations

- Routing of subsidy (mechanism and timeline)
- Equity accounting and statement issuance (quarterly/annual)
- Support obligations (technical assistance, legal aid access)

## 5. Accrual Formula and Vesting Schedule

- Formula: e.g., monthly redirected subsidy × accrual factor × (1 - admin fee)
- Vesting: cliff or graded vesting schedule (e.g., 20% per year for 5 years)

## 6. Default and Cure Periods

- Definition of default (nonpayment, fraud, abandonment)
- Cure period length and process
- Remedies and limited remedies to avoid windfall to private parties

## 7. Exit and Equity Disposition

- Voluntary move: buyout formula or transfer rules
- Eviction: treatment of accrued account (reserve, partial forfeiture)
- Death: succession and transfer to heirs per rules

## 8. Dispute Resolution and Governing Law

- Informal resolution steps
- Arbitration / mediation clause
- Governing law: New York State; venue: New York County

## 9. Article XI / Low-Income Covenants

- Reference to perpetual affordability covenants or HDFC-style restrictions
- Reversionary clauses or right-of-first-refusal for the CLT/cooperative

## 10. Signatures

- Household representative
- Cooperatively-governed entity representative
- HPD or City authorized signer
- Acknowledgment of Right-to-Counsel receipt
