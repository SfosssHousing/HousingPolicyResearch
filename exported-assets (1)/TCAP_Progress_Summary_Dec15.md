   - 24-point homeownership gap driven by policy exclusion (1930s-present)
   - Quantified wealth loss: $156B citywide (NYC)
   - TCAP targeting: Minimum 40% Black/Latino/communities of color
   - 10-year wealth generation: $120 million for 1,500 members
   - Intergenerational multiplier: Next generation inherits homeownership pathways

3. **Financial Sustainability**
   - Year 1-2 cost: $33,100/unit annually
   - Year 5+ cost: $18,000/unit annually
   - Break-even: Year 5 (cumulative subsidy investment = permanent asset)
   - 10-year ROI: $2.375 billion net benefit vs. perpetual rental subsidy

4. **Democratic Governance**
   - Structural safeguards: One-member-one-vote; term limits; recall authority
   - Engagement safeguards: 30-hour training; monthly meetings; quarterly assemblies
   - Permanence safeguards: 30-year covenant; prohibited deconversion; Fair Housing protections

**Section 2.2: COPA as Foundation (Gap Analysis)**
- COPA provides: 45-day notice + 90-day exclusive negotiation for nonprofits
- COPA does NOT provide:
  - Acquisition financing (gap ‚Üí TCAP Tenant Opportunity Fund fills)
  - Permanent affordability covenant (gap ‚Üí TCAP ¬ß 26-1805 fills)
  - Tenant member ownership structure (gap ‚Üí TCAP ¬ß 26-1804 fills)
  - Equity accumulation mechanism (gap ‚Üí TCAP ¬ß 26-1803 fills)
  - Subsidy integration pathway (gap ‚Üí TCAP DSS coordination fills)

**Section 2.3: Integration Mechanisms (Timeline + Financing)**
- **Timeline:** 6-7 months from COPA notice to cooperative closing (135 days COPA negotiation + parallel TCAP recruitment/certification)
- **Financing:** Tenant Opportunity Fund provides 4 products:
  - Pre-development financing: $50-150K (0% interest; non-recourse)
  - Acquisition financing: Up to 30% of purchase price (2-3% interest)
  - Bridge financing: 6-18 months (3-4% interest)
  - Capital improvement: Up to 25% of equity value (2-3% interest)
- **Subsidy Coordination:** DSS automatic allocation (80% to carrying charge, 20% to equity)

**Section 2.4: Policy Gaps TCAP Closes (Comparative Tables)**
