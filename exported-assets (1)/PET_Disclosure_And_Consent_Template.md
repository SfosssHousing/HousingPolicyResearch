# PET_Disclosure_And_Consent_Template

## Summary Box (2-paragraph plain-language)
- What PET is: brief plain-language description of equity accrual from redirected subsidy.
- What changes for you: payment flow, governance role, and what you can expect in 1, 5, and 10 years.

## Key Benefits
- Potential equity accrual, enhanced stability, resident governance

## Key Risks
- Federal subsidy volatility, assessments, limited resale upside, Article XI restrictions, foreclosure risk

## Not Market-Rate Ownership Notice
- Statement clarifying this is controlled, limited-appreciation ownership, not unrestricted market ownership.

## Right to Independent Legal Advice
- Recommendation and confirmation checkbox that RTC was offered/available

## Acknowledgment and Consent
- Signature block, date, and witness/notary line where required
