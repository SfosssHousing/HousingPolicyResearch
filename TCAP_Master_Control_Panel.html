Executive Summary: TCAP
Core Innovation

The Tenant Co-op Acquisition Program (TCAP) transforms NYC's rental subsidy infrastructure from a rent-centered paradigm into one rooted in collective tenant ownership and intergenerational wealth building.

Key Question

How might New York City reconceptualize and structurally redesign its housing subsidy infrastructure to facilitate not only affordability but actual tenant ownership, thereby enabling sustainable intergenerational wealth accumulation within historically disenfranchised and racially marginalized communities?

Four-Phase Conversion Model
Phase 1: Preliminary Organizing & Legal Structuring

Tenant associations establish or affiliate with a legal co-op body. Initial training on cooperative principles, NYC housing law, and governance requirements.

  • Co-op bylaws finalized
  • Board orientation completed
  • Legal representation assigned
Phase 2: Acquisition & Financing Execution

Title transfer and cooperative incorporation occur through HPD-certified acquisition loans, reviewed by NYC Law Dept. and housing court.

  • Property appraisal & inspections
  • Financing secured (city-backed loans)
  • Legal closing & title transfer
  • Building placed under co-op ownership
Phase 3: Subsidy Phase-In & Governance Training

During the 5-year bridge period, rent subsidies are redirected into cooperative obligations and reserves. HPD oversees compliance via fiscal intermediary.

  • Financial literacy modules (budgeting, capital reserves)
  • Cooperative governance training (board duties, meeting procedures)
  • Annual compliance audits to ensure affordability covenants upheld
  • Share equity accrual tracked and documented
Phase 4: Post-Transition Autonomy

Co-ops operate independently with affordability covenants and periodic compliance reports to the city, but retain full democratic governance.

  • Co-op manages operations, maintenance, finances
  • Affordability resale restrictions enforced
  • City receives annual compliance documentation
  • Tenant board makes all strategic decisions
