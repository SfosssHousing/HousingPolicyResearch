## Risk Radar Summary

### Categories

- **Fiscal:** Budget sustainability, no-new-net-cost constraint, federal subsidy volatility.
- **Legal:** Article XI perpetuity, Title VI/FHA disparate impact, ADA/504 compliance, credit-union authority.
- **Operational:** Tenant org capacity, cooperative governance, payment flows, default management.
- **Political:** Agency resistance, tenant skepticism, mayoral continuity, City Council appetite.
